SUPER CARS

A supercar – also called exotic car – is loosely defined or described as a street-legal, luxury superlative performance sports car – both in terms of power, speed, and handling.
The term 'supercar' is therefore frequently used for low-bodied sportscars with powerful, rear mid-mounted engines.
[1] Since the 2000s, the term hypercar has also come into use for the most high-end performance cars.
